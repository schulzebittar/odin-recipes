Strogonoff

Description

This is strogonoff, it goes really well with rice and potato chips. Mostly.

Ingredients

  • Diced beef
  • Ketchup
  • Sour cream
  • Rice/chips

Preparation Steps

  1. Dice the beef
  2. Season it and heat it up in a pan
  3. Add ketchup and sour cream
  4. Mix until homogenous
  5. Serve with rice and chips
