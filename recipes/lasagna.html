Lasagna

Description

This is a Lasagna, it's made out of pasta, meat, tomato sauce and cheese. Mostly.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Meat
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese

Preparation Steps

  1. Season the meat
  2. Cook the pasta strips
  3. Add meat, cover with tomato sauce and cheese
  4. Finish layering with a pasta strip
  5. Repeat until container is full
  6. Bake it in an oven until it looks good
  7. Serve and enjoy
